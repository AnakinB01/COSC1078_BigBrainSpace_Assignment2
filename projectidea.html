Project Idea

Overview:

The IT project we have decided to proceed with as a group entails creating a web based application where users can access 3D renderings and VR models of interior designs for commercial and residential housing applications. These models will be accessible for the user to view from a VR headset or through their own device via an on screen view. The VR headsets will be available for purchase and are also in local Real Estates where clients are able to view property instead of travelling. We will also have options for clients who are constructing their property. They will be required to submit the floorplan of the house, which includes the measurements and labelling of rooms, and also the specifications for their interior (if they also want to see how their house might look after its completed).

Motivation:

Due to the recent events of Covid-19 and the hussle of our clients, we are motivated to create a Virtual house and interior option that can be viewed providing a fully immersive experience like going to the physical location. Due to the travel limits and no house inspections running at the time, our clients found it difficult to make a decision regarding the purchase of the property. This also provides efficiency in general, this will save time for clients as they will not be required to travel long distances.

Description:

The project aims to create a new way of being able to view and create interior designs and houses without the need to go to the physical house. Also people who are building their homes, will be able to check how the colours they have selected will look, and whether that’s how they wanted their house to look. The virtual reality headset is a head mounted device that gives the wearer a whole new experience. The VR headset will be a model of the exterior only and will not demonstrate the interior components and specifications. The VR headset itself will be simple and minimal in design while ensuring it also provides comfort for the users. The material of the headset also has to be durable yet lightweight, the headset can be plastic to prevent it from breaking when hit or dropped. It will include a secure head strap possibly with a rubbery material that supports the back of the head with soft padding to eliminate discomfort, the head strap will be easily adjustable due to the material, the head strap will also be a weight distribution to balance the front heaviness that creates discomfort overtime. The face cover will be a soft material with possibly leather material to prevent soaking of sweat and reduce light leakage. The speakers for the headset will be an over-ear design to provide high quality surround sound that will deliver better immersion and experience. The eye pieces will also be adjustable for the different IPD - interpupillary distance (distancing between the centre of both eyes) of users to create maximum comfort. The features of the VR headset aim to work together to create a minimal and comfortable device that gives an immersive experience for the users to view the modern interior designs stimulating the real world. The Virtual house or interior design will be modern, it will be based on the customer’s requests and their desired floor plan designs and will include all the necessary rooms that’s in a traditional house. The interior design will not only be attractive and pleasing to look at, it will also ensure that it will be fully functional and to proper scale just like a real house. When viewing the interior design, the user will be placed in a virtual house that is a copy of the real house that stimulates the real world. The house will include multiple windows to provide natural lighting and also lights in the house for proper lighting. The house will aim to have a wide colour choice to make features and room look appealing and also the colours will be depended to create the environment of the rooms, the colour choice will be fully customisable from the consumer requests. The rooms will be fully furnished to provide a finished view of the house which can also be changed freely by the customer’s requests The interior designs can also be viewed on a computer or mobile device but without the immersion. By creating this project, it can allow people to go experience houses without ever needing to go to the place. The headset can also be used as a regular VR headset to view movies and play games. The interior designs can provide Real Estates with opportunities such as virtual tours where people can have the option of viewing interior designs without the need to physically go there. This can also bring in opportunities of creating ‘Virtual Holidays’ for people that lack time to travel or unable to travel.

Tools + Tech:

The tools used to create the headset model will be ‘Blender’, which is free 3D computer graphics software for modelling and animating that can render high quality models. The software used to create the interior designs will be using the browser-based platform called ‘Floorplanner’. Floorplanner is a website for people to create interior designs from scratch, it is different from our idea as Floorplanner is essentially like a simplified CAD software that provides you with the tools to create your own designs. The website can create simple VR compatible interior designs with full 3D rendered and designed furniture without modelling designs from scratch. The final rendered designs will be showcased on our website which can be accessed using a VR headset or with a mobile device or pc. The tools used to create the website include the use of software including text editors such as Sublime Text 3 and Visual Studio Code. These text editors allow us to manipulate and add to HTML and CSS files which allow us to populate data and alter styling for our website. We also used multiple text editor extensions like Live Server which allowed us to see real-time changes within the website while we altered the code. Furthermore, we also used Github as a host for our repository where we stored all the HTML and CSS files and used Github Pages to create and host our website through these files. What differentiates us from the floorplanner application is that we act as the ‘middle-man’ in the virtual experience for commercial and residential properties. The floorplanner application requires users to create the interior designs and renderings from scratch, meaning this consumes the time of the customer or real estate agent listing and displaying the renderings and property to clients as they have to develop the design. What we will do and what sets us apart from the Floorplanner application is that we use an architectural plan or current property to design the VR Model in floorplanner without having the Real Estate Agency/ Property Developer making it, then we can display these renderings through our web based application where the users can view their models in VR. If the client does not have access to VR software and the property is being designed or sold through Real Estate Agencies/ Property Development companies, the client/user can go to these companies and view their design through their equipment. This example leads very well into using our application for open houses abroad, for example if someone is moving into another state or area in their country and they cannot attend the open house, they can view the property in VR from the comfort of their home or local real estate agency.

Skills:

Creating the Virtual Reality headset model requires the skill of planning and developing a headset design, it requires research of possible competitor headsets, the materials and the features to provide maximum comfort and immersiveness and also ensuring the model is not only aesthetically pleasing but also considers the functionality and the comfort of the users for the final product. Creating the 3D model with Blender requires the skill of knowing how to model 3D objects with the software from scratch, being able to create the different surfaces and lightings for the final render model and most importantly ensures proper proportions and scaling of the VR headset model. For creating the interior designs, the crucial skills that are necessary is to have ideas and knowledge of colours - what colours creates contrast and how it affects the environment/mood around you. The knowledge of design trends and styles and have to be creative and attention to detail in order to create designs that are eye-catching yet practical that satisfies the consumers. Skills required for ‘Floorplanner’ are knowing how to use the web platform and making sure the interior is to scale to a real floor plan and interior design. There are quite a few skills required for the creation of the website. Firstly, basic knowledge of HTML files and their structure and tags assists the developer in creating a segmented layout with classes and divs for example which can be utilized by CSS to generate a nice layout. Secondly as touched on before, knowledge of CSS and how to manipulate certain attributes allows the user to create an appealing website and thus is key. Thirdly, knowledge of the use of text editors and allocating files to repositories held within GITHUB for example with correct file path referencing within the files is key as this allows the developer to successfully link HTML and CSS together within a repository. Finally, general skills such as knowledge of contrasting font and colour options as well as systematic and effective layout designs help the developer create a more appealing website and thus is a key skill.

Outcome:

The accomplishment of the goal of this project will create a new opportunity for Real Estate Agencies and clients to design their homes and be able to view and experience the interior design in a Virtual Reality headset that will be provided in local Real Estates or at the comfort of your own house with your own Virtual Reality headset or through a web browser. The success of this project will bring about a more efficient and flexible way of conducting property inspections, not just that but it will also save time and money of travelling if the property is located at a distance, especially due to the travel limitations applied during the lockdown. Our project also gives clients the possibility to view the interior of their property, through which they can decide whether that's what they want to proceed with, which will give them maximum satisfaction once their construction is complete. The success of this project will provide a whole new virtual, surreal and immersive experience.
